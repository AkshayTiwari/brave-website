What is the Brave Ad Replacement?

Braveâ€™s goal is to speed up the web, stop bad ads and pay publishers. We plan to accomplish this with ad replacements:

Step One: Brave Blocks All Bad Ads

Why: Advertising has become overrun with ads that slow down the internet, take up ridiculous amounts of space on a page or even deliver malware. Users are responding by installing ad-blockers. A faster, safer web relies on blocking the worst ads - which is what we do.



Step Two: Brave Replaces Ads

We recognize that ads pay for most of our web content. Ads are not going away. So we replace the bad ads with Brave Ads, which we use to pay publishers.



Step Three: Brave Pays You

Replacing ads means you get a share of the gross ad revenue. Brave will pay you 15% of gross ad revenue. This is the same amount of money that we make from those ads (the rest goes to publishers.)
