Browse Faster

Brave blocks everything that can slow you down on the web.

Browse Safer

Brave keeps you and your information safe, effectively sheilding you from 3rd party tracking, fingerprinting and malice.

Make a Difference

Brave creates a bridge between marketers and publishers that brings more revenue to those who make the great content you consume.

Faster than Any Browser on Any Device

We focused on performance by blocking wastefull page code that delays the content on its way to your retinas.
Enjoy up to a 40% increase in page load speed, as well as a major reduction in your data plan burn rate.
Whether browsing on iOS, Mac OS, Windows 10 or Android, pages load faster, saving bandwidth and monthly data usage.


Browse Safer with Brave on Your Side

Brave blocks everything on the web that can cramp your style, and compromise your privacy.
Annoying ads are yesterdays news, and cookies stay in your jar where they belong.

Brave blocks harmful advertising

There's anew ad game in town. It's called "Malvertising". The latest display ad technology can install malware on your laptop without your knowledge. But not with Brave watching your back.

Brave redirects all sites to HTTPS

We've integrated "HTTPS Everywhere" into every Brave browser to make sure you are always moving your bits across the safest possible secure pipe.

Brave blocks Tracking Pixels and Tracking Cookies

Do you ever get that feeling that someone is watching you when you see an ad for something you bought a few days ago? Nothing gets by Brave. We make sure you aren't being tracked while you shop online, and browse your favorite sites.

AdBlockers are gaining ground in the war against the evil tracking empire. It's time to get off this planet and start fresh where the grass is greener and there is more wheat for each one of us. We will save the internet, together.
